How do I become a member?
How do I register?
You can stop by our location preferrably during classes or events and ask one of our volunteers or employees about becoming a member of the makerspace, so you are able to reserve space in the building. We are open all days except for holidays from 7AM to 9PM.
Being a member of CreatorLabs costs $60 a month, and it allows you to use the space and any equipment you need while we're open, as long as you reserve space in advance.

In order to be allowed to use the equipment and various powered tools in the shop, you will first have to take a shop basics class, and get a certificate showing that you have been educated on the proper methods of using the equipment at this makerspace.
